About

I've always had a passion for creating things. My childhood involved a lot of Legos and cardboard castles. When I turned 10 years old my family moved overseas and I was exposed to a new form of design: Scandinavian minimalism.

We explored new cities together and I happily absorbed all the new sensory information. I loved the classical architecture of Rome, the romantic facades of Paris, and the medieval castles of Prague, but I always found myself coming back to the careful and simplistic forms in Danish design. Today, this inspiration manifests itself on the web.

I created this website as a fun personal project, but also as an experiment in optimizing a webpage for speed without sacrificing user experience. New websites frequently place form over function and I wanted to pay homage to old school efficiency with my design.

I continually strive towards improvement. I develop products I believe in by researching carefully, iterating agilely, and supporting my team wholeheartedly.
