My Website

I've always had a passion for creating things. My childhood involved a lot of Legos and cardboard castles. When I was 10 years old my family moved overseas and I was immersed in new cultures and exposed to new ways of thinking. My experience inspires me today and manifests itself on the web.

I created this website as a fun personal project, but also as an experiment in optimizing a webpage for speed without sacrificing user experience. New websites frequently place form over function and I wanted to pay homage to old school efficiency and scandinavian minimalism with my design.
